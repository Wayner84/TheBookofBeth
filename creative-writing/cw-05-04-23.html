Can we do it again?

Published: 5th April 2023

If you said you missed me," she says, "and I told you I missed you too, so you think we could start over?"

"We could relearn each others names and I could tell you about my childhood. You could get bored of my stories all over again and I could get mad like I always used to do"

"And I'm not saying we'd work." She mourners, "I know we probably wouldn't"

"But logic doesn't mean that I don't miss everything about you," she says. "And right know I don't give a damn about what logic has to say. Just tell me you miss me and I swear I'll come running. Tell me you miss me and we can fall all over again."

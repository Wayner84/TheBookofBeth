To the boy who...

Published: 5th February 2021

To the boy who knew me better than I knew myself
To the boy who was there every time I needed help
To the boy who took all of my trust with him

To the boy who left
I was so angry at you
I was so hurt by you
I am still so angry at you
And I'm sick of feeling this way with you
Because truth is, I was so in love with you

And I would never have told you.
I would have kept ignoring the butterflies whenever I'm with you
I would have kept pinching myself whenever you walked into the room
I would have kept turning you into poetry so I can keep flipping through my notebook
And there I would find a thousand different ways to describe your eyes
A thousand different ways to describe your smile
A thousand different ways to describe how I feel every time you walk by
And that's why it hurts.

It hurts in a quiet way
In a way that I felt like you were tugging at my chest
In a way that it felt like you were drowning me.
The memory of you burning into me

its not you, it me

Published: 19th November 2018

"Why did you guys actually break up?" She laughed, almost painfully.

"One day he loved me and the next he didn't. Strange, isn't it? How fast somebody's feelings can change and then there is nothing you can do but accept it. You have to sit and accept the fact that you're completely in love with somebody who doesn't give a toss whether you come or go, and quite frankly they would rather if you didn't come at all. And that really sucks."

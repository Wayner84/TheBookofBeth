It’s a bad day. Not a bad life

Published: 23rd October 2023

Sometimes a bad day turns into a bad week, which finally becomes a bad month, which ends up leading to generalised bad times. And you struggle. You feel like you’ve been thrown into a battlefield you most certainly did not ask for. But you are here, and you have to keep fighting. Sometimes, staying alive is like taking a spoon of the medicine you hate the most. The syrup solution is bitter, hard to swallow and straight up disgusting so you block your nose with your other hand and drink it anyway because you have no other option. The same thing happens here. When you don’t want to fight anymore because you are just so tired, all you have to do is blindly trust you’re gonna make it out alive. Even if you are crawling on the floor, I don’t care, you’ve got to keep fighting. Life goes on after death, you will go on after bad times, I swear.

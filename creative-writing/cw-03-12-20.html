Little snippets that may progress to more

Published: 3rd December 2020

Is that why you play the music so loud? A beat to drown out the thoughts, sounds so high you cannot think, lyrics so close to home you don't even blink.

If you haven't felt like quitting, your dreams aren't big enough.

It's 12am and her mind wanders. She tends to think about something that happened 5 years ago or something that happened 2 hours ago. Or even something that could happen 10 years from now. Her mind is like a hurricane, it's a wreck. It's beautiful yet awful thoughts. It's now 1am and her mind continues to wander.

Your first love isn't the first person you give your heart to - it's the first person who breaks it.

Truth is, no matter how strong you are, at some point you will hit rock bottom, and it will feel like the world is collapsing beneath your feet; but the only person who can get you back on the surface is yourself.

For you I was just like the last part of a cigarette that you throw on the street and leave to die, you ignited me with your words but killed me with your actions.

After he broke my heart, I began writing about him. I wrote and I wrote and I've described him as a hurricane, a drug, my universe. Now that I've moved on, I don't see him in that way anymore. He wasn't anything above ordinary, he was just a boy. A boy who didn't want to be with me and that's that.

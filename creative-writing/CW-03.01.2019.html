It still hurts...

Published: 3rd January 2019

Of course it hurts to know you lost total interest in me, kind of like a slap in the face. It also does pain me to walk past you in the corridor pretending we never met and stayed up all night talking. It feels like a knife through my chest every time I think about it. What truly hurt the most, so bad I can't even find the words to explain it, is that you left me with no explanation! And I keep waiting for a sign or something, anything, from you, but maybe I should accept that sometimes, no message is also a message in itself.

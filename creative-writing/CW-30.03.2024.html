in case you needed to hear this…

Published: 30th March 2024

You can love someone who treats you like shit. You can love someone who makes you feel worthless. You can love someone who broke your heart. You can love someone who was never even yours in the first place. You can love someone who doesn’t deserve you at all.

You might still have strong feelings for your ex, but don’t let your heart convince you to give him another chance when he has already run out of them. Don’t chase after someone who does not want to be caught. Don’t run back to the past because it’s familiar. Let yourself move on from him. Let yourself love someone else, someone who treats you right this time.

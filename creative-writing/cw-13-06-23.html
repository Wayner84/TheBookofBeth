I just want to be enough

Published: 13th June 2023

I wish I could be the first person you think about when you wake up and the last when you go to sleep. I wish you smiled at your phone whenever my name pops up, just like I do when yours does. I wish you wanted to see me just as much as I want to see you. I wish you could see in yourself what I see in you. I wish you didn't hurt the way that you do and that I could take some of it away for you. I wish you could talk to me about the things that upset you and instead of bottling it up and saying "I'm fine" you would be honest with me.

I wish that one day I can be your everything, and I wish one day that I can be enough for you.

I wish you wanted that too but I'll just never be enough for you.

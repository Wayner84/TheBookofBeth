I loved him once

Published: 16th November 2018

"Does it still hurt?"

"Yes, and I think it always will, at least a little."

"But you said you didn't love him anymore?"

"I don't, but I used to. He had my whole heart in his hands. He was so gentle while he cared, but when he stopped caring, he completely crushed it, into a million tiny pieces, and it won't ever be the same again. I gave him everything, and he won't give it back. He's chosen somebody else, even though he already had every single piece of me. But, in time, I will heal. Probably not completely, but to the point where it only hurts on the really bad days. And I'm determined to only have a few of those!"

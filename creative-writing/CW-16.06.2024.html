I’m finally happy

Published: 16th June 2024

It’s been one month and it just hit me that you aren’t coming back for me. It’s like a blow to the head, leaving me dizzy and hurting like hell.

It’s been two months and I wished you a happy birthday. You said thank you and didn’t talk to me after that.

It’s been three months and I heard about your new girl. Of course she was the one I heard rumours of you having g a thing with while you were with me. I shouldn’t have ignored them.

It’s been four months and I hate you, yet I’m still hoping you’ll leave her and come back to me.

It’s been five months and you started talking to me again, bringing up old memories of been trying to bury.

It’s been six months and I am beginning to move on. I like someone else, but I’m not sure what to do yet.

It’s been seven months and I heard you’re not with your girl anymore. I feel nothing.

It’s been eight months and you’re trying to win me back, but I’ve realised how pointless you are. I don’t care about you anymore.

It’s been nine months and I’m doing great, while it has just got you that you’ve lost me.

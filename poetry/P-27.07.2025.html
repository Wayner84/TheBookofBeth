What doesn’t kill you, doesn’t make you stronger

Published: 27th July 2025

What doesn’t kill you makes you stronger is what say,
But my experiences made me dangerously close to taking my life away,
It didn’t make me stronger,
It made me not able to eat,
And feel so dizzy that sometimes I couldn’t even stand on my feet,
And made me have panic attacks,
In fact it became a disorder,
A daily handful of grief,
Shaking and crying,
Wanting nothing but relief,
I will say it again,
Although it’s already been said,
It didn’t make me stronger,
It made me think I’d be better off dead,
And caused me sleepless night after sleepless night,
With so many tears that sometimes it felt like I was going to lose my sight,
So no,
What doesn’t kill you doesn’t always make you stronger,
Sometimes it makes you weak and gasping for air,
Physically not able to speak,
And that’s okay because it takes time for sorrow to fade away.

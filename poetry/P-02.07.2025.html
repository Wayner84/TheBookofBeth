Poetry

I’m so lucky

Published: 2nd July 2025

How lucky am I that my path crossed with yours,
That I found you without looking,
That something greater guided us in the same direction,
At the same time,
And it lead us to the greatest thing of all,
Knowing each other,
And the thing about life is,
We have almost every day,
More than we could ever imagine,
Parallel existences with such small gaps between them,
You need a magnifying glass to spot the space,
But you and I had a fated encounter,
We intertwined,
And I don’t remember who I was,
I’m the moments leading up to our lives intersecting,
But I was never the same again after that,
Meeting you felt like finally reading the instructions,
After trying for so long to figure it out on my own,
It felt like the moment when you’re studying for a test,
And it finally starts making sense,
Like finishing a puzzle,
And I don’t know if people are magnetically meant to keep pulling toward one another until they finally connect,
Or if souls just float around incomplete finding their missing pieces,
But on a day where I wasn’t even using a map,
I didn’t even know I was lost,
Our paths crossed and you helped me get home,
And for that I will always feel lucky.

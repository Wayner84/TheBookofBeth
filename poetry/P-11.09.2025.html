Poetry

There’s good sadness too, I swear

Published: 11th September 2025

I think sadness gets a bad rep,
Always avoided like the long way back,
But there’s good sadness too,
Like when its Sunday evening and Monday looms,
When you have to say goodbye for a long time to the people you love,
You can measure the love felt in the length of a hug.

Good sadness is a hard conversation with a good friend,
It’s being there at the bedside when life comes to an end,
Good sadness is grief after years,
Spontaneous tears because you wish you could just have one more cheer,
With that one friend you don’t see anymore.

There’s good sadness too,
I swear,
It’s post holiday blues,
It’s how the wind feels in the summer air,
Missing people that aren’t there,
It’s knowing that they’ll never truly know how much you care.

There’s good sadness,
Its not always bad,
Sometimes sadness is a reminder of everything you have.
